Job Description

Your Career

Your Career Our Information Security team is seeking an early-career Technical Program Manager to coordinate and implement information security projects. This role is ideal for a motivated individual who thrives in dynamic environments, embraces continuous learning, exhibits a positive attitude, and excels at teamwork.

Your Impact

  • Improve and automate processes for the technical program management team.
  • Lead smaller-scale security initiatives across various teams.
  • Develop and present key metrics in accessible formats to measure the success of strategic programs and initiatives.
  • Author clear functional specifications for critical projects to facilitate effective technical discussions among stakeholders.
  • Gain deep understanding of the relevant systems, including their limitations, scalability, constraints, and architectural rationale.

Qualifications

Your Experience
  • 2-3 years of experience as a software developer or QA engineer in a sizable engineering team.
  • Basic understanding and experience in technical program management.
  • Proven ability to manage and prioritize team backlogs.
  • Excellent skills in delivering clear, concise, transparent, and insightful status updates to team members, executives, and diverse stakeholders.
  • Skilled in resolving project conflicts and establishing effective resolution paths.
  • Working knowledge of Python, Java, Shell scripting, and awareness of emerging AI technologies.
  • Working knowledge of tools like JIRA (or similar bug tracking software) and Tableau (or comparable data visualization tools).

Education
  • Bachelor's degree from an accredited four-year institution or equivalent training, education, and experience in Information Security, Cybersecurity, Computer Systems, IT, or related fields.

Additional Information

Be part of a dynamic, energetic team that embraces challenges and passionately resolves security vulnerabilities to protect privacy.
